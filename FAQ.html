Frequently Asked Questions - Admission

Find answers to common questions about our admission process.

1. What are the admission requirements? +
Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, applicants need to provide academic transcripts, standardized test scores, and a personal statement.
2. When is the application deadline? +
The deadlines depend on the program. Typically, fall admission deadlines are in early spring, while spring admissions close in the fall.
3. How can I apply for financial aid? +
You can apply for financial aid through the university's financial aid office. Scholarships, grants, and loan options are available.
4. Is there an application fee? +
Yes, most programs require an application fee. However, fee waivers may be available for eligible applicants.
5. Can I apply as an international student? +
Yes, international students are welcome to apply. Additional requirements include English proficiency tests and visa documentation.
